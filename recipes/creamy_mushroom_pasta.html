Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Description

Creamy Mushroom Pasta is a luscious and comforting dish that combines the earthy richness of mushrooms with a velvety, decadent cream sauce. This pasta creation is a testament to the marriage of simple yet sophisticated flavors. Typically featuring a variety of mushrooms such as cremini or wild mushrooms, the dish is elevated by sautéing them to enhance their natural savory qualities.

The creamy sauce, often made with a base of heavy cream or a combination of cream and chicken or vegetable broth, brings a luxurious texture and a delightful richness to the pasta. Garlic, shallots, or onions are commonly used to infuse additional depth of flavor. The pasta of choice, whether it's fettuccine, linguine, or another favorite, serves as the perfect canvas to absorb the velvety sauce and complement the hearty mushrooms.

Garnishes like fresh parsley, grated Parmesan, or a sprinkle of black pepper may be added for a finishing touch. Creamy Mushroom Pasta is a versatile and satisfying dish, perfect for both casual weeknight dinners and more elegant occasions, offering a harmonious blend of creamy, umami goodness that appeals to pasta lovers everywhere.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 250g button chestnut mushroom, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, finely grated
  • 100ml dry white wine
  • 200ml double cream
  • 1 lemon, zest only
  • 200g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), grated, plus extra to serve
  • 300g tagliatelle or linguini
  • ½ small bunch parsley, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. Step

    2. Heat the oil and butter in a medium saucepan. Fry the onion over a low heat for 10 mins or until softened and translucent.

  2. Step

    3. Add the mushrooms and cook for 10 mins over a medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 2 mins. Add the wine and bring to a simmer, reduce the liquid by half.

  3. Step

    4. Add the double cream and bring to a simmer, then add the lemon zest and parmesan. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper.

  4. Step

    5. Meanwhile, cook the pasta following pack instructions. Reserve 100ml of the pasta water. Toss the pasta in the pan with the creamy sauce and enough of the reserved water to loosen. Stir through the parsley, divide into bowls and top with extra cheese, if you like.
