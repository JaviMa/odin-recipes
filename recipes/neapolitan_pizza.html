Neapolitan Pizza

Description

Neapolitan pizza, a culinary gem originating from Naples, Italy, represents the pinnacle of pizza craftsmanship. Known for its simple yet exquisite composition, it features a thin and soft crust, adorned with traditional toppings like fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella di bufala, basil leaves, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. The dough, crafted from high-quality "00" flour, undergoes meticulous preparation, often requiring hours of fermentation for optimal flavor and texture. Baked quickly in a wood-fired oven at high temperatures, Neapolitan pizza achieves a perfect balance of chewiness, crispiness, and a delightful charred edge. Recognized as a UNESCO cultural heritage, the Neapolitan pizza embodies centuries of culinary tradition, offering a sensory experience that transcends borders and captivates pizza enthusiasts worldwide.

Neapolitan Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Neapolitan pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce (made with fresh tomatoes, salt, and basil)
  • Fresh buffalo mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature, preferably with a pizza stone if available.
  2. Roll out a dough ball into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or a floured tray.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving an uncovered border.
  5. Place pieces of buffalo mozzarella on top of the sauce.
  6. Bake the pizza in the hot oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the mozzarella is bubbly.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil just before serving.

Enjoy your authentic Neapolitan Margherita pizza!
